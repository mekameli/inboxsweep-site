Description of Service

InboxSweep is a native macOS application that connects to your Gmail account to help you:

  • Categorize emails using on-device AI
  • Archive emails locally to your Mac
  • Remove unwanted emails by moving them to Gmail Trash
  • Restore archived emails back to your Gmail inbox

Your Gmail Account

Authentication

  • InboxSweep accesses your Gmail through Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol
  • You authorize specific permissions when you connect your account
  • You can revoke access at any time through Google Account settings

Your Data, Your Control

  • InboxSweep processes your emails only on your Mac — never on external servers
  • You choose which emails to archive, remove, or restore
  • InboxSweep never permanently deletes emails — all removals go to Gmail Trash (recoverable for 30 days)

What InboxSweep Does and Does Not Do

InboxSweep Does:

  • Read your emails for display and categorization
  • Move emails to Gmail Trash when you choose to archive or remove them
  • Store archived emails as local files on your Mac
  • Upload emails back to Gmail when you choose to restore them

InboxSweep Does Not:

  • Send your emails to any external server
  • Permanently delete any emails
  • Access your Gmail without your explicit authorization
  • Share your data with any third party
  • Display advertising or track your behavior

Local Archive

When you archive emails through InboxSweep:

  • A complete copy is saved to your Mac's local storage
  • The email is moved to Gmail Trash (recoverable for 30 days)
  • Your local copy is permanent and remains even if Gmail permanently deletes the trashed email
  • You can restore emails from your local archive back to Gmail at any time
