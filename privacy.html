Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

TL;DR: InboxSweep processes your emails entirely on your Mac. We have no servers that store your email. We can't read your mail because it never reaches us.

Overview

InboxSweep is a native macOS application that helps Gmail users organize and clean up their inboxes. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and how we protect it.

What Data We Access

InboxSweep accesses your Gmail account data through Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication with the following permissions:

  • Read email messages — to scan, categorize, and display your emails
  • Modify email labels — to move emails to Gmail Trash when you choose to archive or remove them
  • Import messages — to restore previously archived emails back to your Gmail inbox

How We Use Your Data

On Your Device Only

All email processing happens locally on your Mac. Your emails are never sent to our servers or any third-party service. Email classification uses on-device AI (Apple's NLEmbedding framework) — no email content is transmitted anywhere. Archived emails are stored as local files on your Mac's hard drive.

What We Do

  • Read your email metadata (sender, subject, date, labels) for categorization
  • Read email content for display within the app
  • Download raw email files (.eml) to your Mac when you choose to archive
  • Move emails to Gmail Trash when you choose to archive or remove them
  • Restore archived emails to your Gmail inbox when you request it

What We Never Do

  • We never send your email content to any external server
  • We never permanently delete your emails (all removals go to Gmail Trash, recoverable for 30 days)
  • We never sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties
  • We never store your data on any server — everything stays on your Mac
  • We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics

Optional Cloud Features

If you choose to enable "Azure Embeddings" in Settings, email sender names, subject lines, and the first 200 characters of previews are sent to your own Azure endpoint for AI classification. This is opt-in only and disabled by default.

Data Retention

  • Archived emails remain on your Mac until you manually delete them from the Archive
  • Scanned email cache is stored locally and updated each time you scan
  • OAuth tokens are stored in the macOS Keychain and removed when you sign out
  • Uninstalling the app removes all application data

Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Sign out — removes all stored authentication tokens
  • Delete your local archive — removes all locally stored emails
  • Revoke access — go to myaccount.google.com/permissions to revoke InboxSweep's access to your Gmail
  • Request data deletion — uninstalling the app removes all local data. No server-side data exists to delete.

Google API Services User Data Policy

InboxSweep's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Children's Privacy

InboxSweep is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
