How it works

Three steps to a
clean inbox.

No complex setup, no cloud accounts. Just connect, classify, and clean.

1

Connect Gmail

Sign in with your Google account using OAuth 2.0. InboxSweep never stores your password — only a secure token in your Mac's Keychain.

2

AI Classifies

Apple's on-device NL framework reads and classifies your inbox in seconds. No internet required — everything runs on your chip.

3

Clean Up

Archive entire categories, clean up by sender, or review what needs your attention. Take back your inbox in minutes.

Privacy

Your emails never
leave your Mac.

Most email apps send your data to the cloud. InboxSweep does the opposite. Every byte stays on your machine — processed by on-device AI, stored in your own filesystem, protected by FileVault and your macOS Keychain.

Read the full Privacy Policy →
🔒
No server, no cloud
InboxSweep has no backend. Your emails are never transmitted to any server — ours or anyone else's.
🧠
On-device AI only
Classification uses Apple's NLEmbedding framework — it runs entirely on your Mac's CPU/GPU, offline.
🗝️
OAuth tokens in Keychain
Your Gmail credentials are stored only in macOS Keychain, encrypted by the system. Sign out removes them instantly.
♻️
Nothing is permanent
Archived emails go to Gmail Trash — recoverable for 30 days. Your local archive persists until you delete it.
