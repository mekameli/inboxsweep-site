How InboxSweep helps

Your inbox is overwhelming.
It doesn't have to be.

InboxSweep uses on-device AI to identify what matters, what's junk, and what's been sitting there for years — then makes it effortless to clean up, without losing a single email.

AI identifies the clutter

On-device AI classifies every email into 10 smart categories — marketing, social, newsletters, finance, work, personal, and more. No manual sorting. No rules to write. It just works.

Clean up in bulk, by category or sender

See 300 marketing emails from the last 3 years? Select all, archive in one click. Group by sender to find who's flooding your inbox. Clean thousands of emails in minutes, not hours.

Every email archived locally — nothing is ever lost

Before anything is removed from Gmail, a complete copy is saved on your Mac. Worried you might need an email later? It's right there in your local archive — searchable, readable, restorable to Gmail with one click.

AI-powered search finds anything instantly

Search by meaning, not just keywords. Type "payment" and find invoices, receipts, and billing emails too. Semantic search works across your inbox and local archive — even when you're offline.

Export your entire archive to an external drive

One-click export creates a portable archive — organized .eml files by category, a universal MBOX file, and full metadata. Back up to an external drive, NAS, or cloud storage. Your emails, your way, forever.

Nothing is ever permanently deleted

InboxSweep doesn't delete emails. When you clean up, emails move to Gmail Trash (recoverable for 30 days) while your local archive keeps a permanent copy. Peace of mind, built into every action.

Simple by design

From cluttered to clean
in three steps.

No configuration. No cloud accounts. No learning curve.

1

Connect your Gmail

Sign in securely with Google OAuth. InboxSweep never sees your password — it uses a secure token stored in your Mac's Keychain. Choose what to scan: all mail, inbox only, or specific time ranges.

2

AI analyzes everything

On-device AI reads every email and sorts it into smart categories. See instantly how many are marketing, which need your attention, and where the biggest cleanup opportunities are. Watch results stream in live as emails are processed.

3

Clean up with confidence

Select a category, hit archive, and watch hundreds of emails get safely stored on your Mac and moved to Gmail Trash. Search for any email later with AI-powered semantic search. Export your entire archive to an external drive anytime. Nothing is ever lost.

Privacy first

Your emails never leave your Mac.

Unlike other email tools that upload your data to their servers, InboxSweep processes everything locally. The AI runs on Apple's built-in NLEmbedding framework — no internet connection required for classification or search.

On-device only

All AI processing happens on your Mac's chip. Your emails are never uploaded to any external server — not ours, not anyone's.

Nothing sold or shared

We don't sell your data, share it with third parties, or use it for advertising. Your email content belongs to you and stays with you.

No AI training on your data

Your emails are never used to train machine learning models. The on-device AI uses Apple's pre-trained models — your data never improves anyone else's product.

You control everything

Revoke access anytime from Google Account settings. Uninstalling removes all local data. Your archive is yours to export, move, or delete at will.
