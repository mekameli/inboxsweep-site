Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built for people who value their time and their privacy.

🔒

Private by Default

All email processing happens on your Mac using Apple's on-device AI. Your emails never touch a server. Ever.

🗂️

Smart Categories

Newsletters, receipts, notifications, social — InboxSweep sorts your mail into categories so you can deal with them in bulk.

📦

Archive & Protect

Save emails as local .eml files on your Mac before cleaning up. Your archive is yours — no cloud required.

↩️

Restore Anytime

Changed your mind? Restore any archived email back to your Gmail inbox with one click. No data ever lost.

📊

Storage Insights

See what's eating your Gmail storage. Spot large attachments and old threads that are wasting space.

🛡️

Never Deletes

InboxSweep only moves emails to Gmail Trash — never permanent deletion. Everything is recoverable for 30 days.

How It Works

Clean inbox in three steps.

Getting started takes under a minute.

1

Connect Gmail

Sign in with your Google account. InboxSweep requests only the permissions it needs — you can revoke access anytime from your Google settings.

2

Scan & Categorize

InboxSweep scans your inbox using on-device AI. Emails are grouped into categories like Newsletters, Receipts, Notifications, and more.

3

Clean Up

Review each category and decide: archive locally, move to trash, or keep. Bulk actions make it fast. Your local archive keeps a copy safe.

Privacy

Your emails stay on
your Mac. Period.

We don't have servers that store your email. We can't read your mail because it never reaches us.

🖥️

On-device processing

AI categorization runs entirely on your Mac using Apple's NLEmbedding framework. No cloud calls.

🚫

No external servers

InboxSweep has no backend. Your emails are never transmitted to any server we control.

🔑

Tokens in Keychain

OAuth tokens are stored securely in the macOS Keychain — never in plain text or iCloud sync.

📂

Archive stays local

Archived emails live in your Mac's Application Support folder. Delete the app, delete the data.

🗑️

Trash, not delete

InboxSweep never permanently deletes email. Everything goes to Gmail Trash — recoverable for 30 days.

📋

Google API compliance

Full compliance with the Google API Services User Data Policy and Limited Use requirements.
